Project Overview

Rwandaful app is a platform that highlights the beauty of rwanda, from destinations, events, things to do, people and cultures.

Background

Rwanda is located in east africa. It has green mountain landscape. This place is known for its flaura and fauna. It is a home for many rare species like mountain gorillas and golden monkey, which are mainly found in Volcanoes National Park. The capital of this country is Kigali, which is known for its vibrant nightlife and a variety of bars and restaurants. There are a number of places to see in Rwanda. Some of the must visit places here are Volcanoes National Park, Kigali Memorial Centre, Inema Art Centre, Ntarana Church, Nyamata Church, Virunga National Park and Akegara National Park to include some. Rwanda Beaches are also very popular among the tourists.

Challenges

Tourism in Rwanda is not so great. There are few notable challenges thousands of tourist face while exploring the country

  • Lack of comprehensive informations about destinations, events, accomodation & things to do
  • Difficulty finding travel agencies
  • Lack of budget idead & planning for vacation
  • Not enough local information about the people & their cultures
  • Expensive travel packages online
  • Fraudulent agencies online

Solutions

Create a platform that provides comprehensive information about places to visit, accomodations & things to do in rwanda

  • A platform that showcases budget friendly packages, compare similar packages from different agencies to get the best deals
  • Difficulty finding travel agencies
  • A platform that lists verified agencies and their contact informations
  • Provide enough local informations about the history, culture, people, beliefs & way of life of the visiting destination
